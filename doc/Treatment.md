### Opening Hook (0:00–0:30)

Black screen.

> "I don't think we know what small language models are really for."

Pause.

> "So how do you build tools for use cases that haven't been discovered yet?"

Cut to the DoubleNaught logo.

Let us get our feet wet in this whole SLM business by taking a use case that we do have and discovering what we can do with that.   

I want the SLM to generate images in the style of WM.   I want it to generate from a prompt. I want it to generate from an image in combination with a prompt.  I want it to maintain consistency in style after the original art work.  

What to do first?  Collect data.  Here our data set is a collection of images.  That is where we start, with the collection.  But we need to condition the images.  We need to break them into smaller parts and label them.
